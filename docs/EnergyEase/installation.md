# App Installation

:::info Prerequisite

Before you start, make sure your device meets the following requirements:

- Operating system: iOS 13.0 or later; Android 5.0 or later.
- Connected to a working Wi-Fi or mobile network.
- Enough free storage space.
- Sufficient battery power, or keep your device plugged in.

:::
